Tapping into the energy and diversity of New York City, Google’s second largest office is a focal point for innovation in fields ranging from computer science to creative branding. Solving programming challenges in a collaborative environment, our engineers play key roles in Google Drive, Search, AdWords, and Maps. Our sales team, one of Google’s biggest, caters to global clients including major media companies and ad agencies. We also developed Crisis Response Maps out of the NYC hub. With easy access to the High Line and breathtaking views, our Chelsea office is designed to inspire.
